## 1. Pricing & Costs

### Q1: Why does a custom React website cost more than a WordPress template?

**A:** A custom React website is built from scratch specifically for your business needs, while WordPress templates are pre-made designs used by thousands of other websites. With a custom React website:
- You get unique design and functionality tailored to your brand
- Better performance and faster loading times
- Enhanced security (no plugin vulnerabilities)
- Scalability for future growth
- No monthly plugin subscription fees
- Full ownership of the code
- Better SEO capabilities
- Modern technology stack that won't become obsolete quickly

While WordPress templates may seem cheaper initially, they often require paid plugins, premium themes, and ongoing maintenance that can add up significantly over time.

### Q2: What are the ongoing costs after the website is launched?

**A:** The ongoing costs are minimal:

- **Maintenance:** ₹5,000-₹15,000/month (optional, based on package selected)
- **No hidden costs:** No plugin subscriptions, no theme renewals, no licensing fees

Total minimum annual cost: ₹600-₹900 (domain only with free hosting)

## 9. Comparison with Alternatives

### Q38: Why not use WordPress?

**A:** While WordPress is popular, custom React offers advantages:
- **Performance:** React is faster, especially for product catalogs
- **Security:** No plugin vulnerabilities
- **Scalability:** Better for growing businesses
- **Customization:** Unlimited flexibility
- **Modern:** Future-proof technology
- **Cost:** No plugin subscriptions over time
- **User Experience:** Smoother, app-like experience

WordPress is better if you need a blog-heavy site or extensive plugin ecosystem.

### Q39: Why not use a website builder (Wix, Squarespace)?

**A:** Custom React vs website builders:
- **Ownership:** You own the code (vs locked into platform)
- **Performance:** Much faster loading times
- **SEO:** Better SEO capabilities
- **Customization:** Unlimited vs limited templates
- **Scalability:** Can grow without platform limitations
- **Cost:** One-time vs monthly platform fees
- **Portability:** Can move anywhere (vs locked to builder)

Website builders are good for simple personal sites, not professional product catalogs.

### Q40: Why not hire an in-house developer?

**A:** Custom development vs in-house:
- **Cost:** One-time project vs monthly salary (₹50,000-₹1,00,000/month)
- **Expertise:** Specialized React expertise vs generalist
- **Time:** 4-6 weeks vs months to hire and train
- **Support:** Professional support vs dependency on one person
- **Risk:** No employee turnover risk vs hiring risk

For a one-time project, hiring us is more cost-effective and faster.
